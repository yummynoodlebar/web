Learn More About Us

At Yummy Noodles Bar, we take pride in serving freshly made Asian dishes inspired by authentic street food across Thailand, Vietnam, and beyond.
  • Prepared daily using high-quality ingredients and traditional recipes.
  • Cooked to order, with rich flavors and bold spices in every bite.

From classic noodle bowls to flavorful stir-fries and aromatic soups, every dish is crafted to satisfy your cravings and bring the taste of Southeast Asia to Eastbourne.
